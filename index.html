Hi! I am Sina Heydari. I hold an MSc in Computer Engineering, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, from the Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences (IASBS).

Research

My research examines learning and inference from user-generated data, with an emphasis on modeling higher-order structure, weak supervision, and interaction-aware representations. My master’s thesis, proposed a multi-level hypergraph framework for automatic personality prediction from text, modeling personality traits as latent structures emerging from hierarchical relationships in user-generated text and capturing higher-order interactions beyond pairwise graphs.
